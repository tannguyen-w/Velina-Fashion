Fabric care rules

To avoid shrinkage, pre-wash natural fibers the same way you plan to wash the finished item, including drying. Wash light and bright colors separately, especially in the first few washes. Take extra care with high-contrast prints (e.g. red, black, navy on light fabric) — cold wash and dry promptly to prevent color bleed. Delicate fabrics like satins, sequined, beaded, foiled, or specialty weights may need special care. Always follow care instructions, as we can’t accept claims for items laundered incorrectly.
