Frequently Asked Question

What is your return and exchange policy?

We accept returns or exchanges within 30 days, as long as items are unworn, in original condition with tags attached. Please contact us for the return address.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location and will be calculated at checkout.

How do I know which size to order?

You can refer to our size guide on each product page. If you're unsure, feel free to contact us — we're happy to help with sizing!

When will my order be shipped?

Orders are usually processed within 1–3 business days. Once shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking details.

Can I make changes to my order after it's been placed?

If your order hasn’t been shipped yet, we’ll do our best to help. Please contact us as soon as possible for any changes.

How should I care for my items?

Each product comes with care instructions on the label. We recommend cold wash and air dry for most garments. For special fabrics like satin or sequins, gentle care is advised.
